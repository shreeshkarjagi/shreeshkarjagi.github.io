Project detail

This experiment was studied to test specimens’ strength and learn about their properties and application using a tensile test. The properties were discussed after drawing the stress-strain curve for each specimen and calculating the elastic Young’s modulus, modulus of resilience, and modulus of toughness. Another part of the experiment was to observe how three specimens prepared of the same material might differ from each other with respect to the location of fracture and propagation of necking during the axial loading. After understanding the properties of these specimens we can then decide what material to use for our chassis.
