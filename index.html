About

Hi! I'm Shreesh Karjagi, a third year Bioengineering student at the University of California, Los Angeles. I am determined to dedicate my life's work towards research, education, and engineering.

Research Engineer & Undergraduate Instructor.

I look to leverage technical problem solving to find innovative answers to the issues we face - be that in healthcare accessibility or enviornmental sustainabilty. This drives me to work on strengthening my understanding of technical concepts while gaining experience with hands-on engineering projects and as a leader in extracurriculars and research.

My interests revolve around reseach and design, product development (primarily in the development of diagnostic tools and therapeutics, and the application of systems biology and data sciences to biomedical problems) and development of green energy systems (energy generation, energy storage system, smart grid systems and grid integration, water and wastewater treatment)

The ultimate goal of this project is to design and construct a hydrogen fuel cell tricycle that people can drive safely on the road. This experiment was studied to test specimens’ strength and learn about their properties and application using a tensile test.
