About

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Shreesh Karjagi, and I'm a senior in engineering at UCLA. I'm passionate about developing innovative solutions to real-world challenges, and I have international experience in a range of fields, including bioengineering, water and wastewater treatment, research and design, project management, analysis, and report writing. I've had the privilege of working with multidisciplinary teams to translate stakeholder requirements into technology and process upgrades, and I find it incredibly rewarding to collaborate with others in this way. My ability to work effectively across cultures and high EQ are skills that I value greatly. I'm excited to share my engineering journey with you and explore ways to make a positive impact on the world through innovation.

Research Engineer & Undergraduate Instructor.

My passion lies in leveraging technical problem-solving skills to find innovative solutions to complex challenges in areas such as healthcare accessibility and environmental sustainability. To achieve this, I am dedicated to expanding my understanding of technical concepts through hands-on engineering projects, as well as leadership roles in extracurricular activities and research.

My interests revolve around research and design, with a focus on product development. Specifically, I am interested in the development of diagnostic tools and therapeutics using systems biology and data sciences to address biomedical problems. Additionally, I have a strong interest in the development of water and wastewater treatment systems.
